Our Story

Experience Colorado Springs' Nightlife Underground

Hidden beneath the bustling streets of downtown Colorado Springs lies a world unlike any other. The Rabbit Hole is an underground sanctuary where Wonderland-inspired ambiance meets upscale New American cuisine.

Descend the staircase and step into a realm of gothic elegance, flickering candlelight, and meticulously crafted cocktails. Every detail - from the velvet seating to the curated playlist - is designed to transport you somewhere extraordinary.

Open daily, we invite you to lose yourself in an experience that blurs the line between dinner and wonderland.
